Ripple Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Ripple is a mood journal, habit tracking, and AI companion app. Ripple is not a medical device, emergency service, diagnosis tool, or substitute for professional healthcare.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, Ripple may collect and process:

  • Account information such as username, optional email, display name, avatar, age group, and city.
  • Journal entries, mood scores, selected mood factors, photos, and audio that you choose to upload.
  • Health and wellness data that you choose to sync, including step counts, sleep sessions, water intake, and meditation sessions.
  • AI chat messages and derived safety or wellness insights used to personalize the in-app experience.
  • Feedback that you submit through the app.

Health Connect and Health Data

On Android, Ripple requests Health Connect read access for Steps and Sleep only when you ask the app to sync those metrics. Ripple uses this data to show daily and historical wellness summaries inside the app. Ripple does not request Health Connect write access, does not sell health data, and does not use health data for advertising.

How We Use Information

Ripple uses information to:

  • Provide account, journal, tracking, notification, and AI chat features.
  • Personalize encouragement, summaries, and insights.
  • Store user-uploaded media and restore it in the app.
  • Send password reset emails when requested.
  • Maintain safety, security, debugging, and service reliability.

Storage and Third-Party Services

Ripple stores app data in a backend database. Uploaded media may be stored through Cloudinary. AI chat responses may be generated through a third-party AI provider. Password reset emails may be sent through Gmail SMTP. These services process data only to provide Ripple features.

Security

Ripple uses authentication, server-side access controls, and app-side secure storage where appropriate. Some journal media may be encrypted before upload. No internet service can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can choose not to provide optional information, decline Health Connect permissions, disable health syncing, edit your profile, delete journal entries, or request account deletion.

Children

Ripple is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can remove it.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact support@ripple.app.
